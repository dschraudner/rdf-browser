Henri (French: Henri Albert Gabriel Félix Marie Guillaume, pronounced [ɑ̃ˈʀi]; born 16 April 1955) is the Grand Duke of Luxembourg, reigning since 7 October 2000. He is the eldest son of Grand Duke Jean and Princess Joséphine-Charlotte of Belgium, and a first cousin of King Philippe of Belgium. In 2019, his net worth was estimated around US$4 billion.
